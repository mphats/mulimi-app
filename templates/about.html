About Mlimi App

Empowering farmers in Malawi with AI-driven agricultural solutions

Our Mission

Mlimi App is dedicated to revolutionizing agriculture in Malawi by providing farmers with cutting-edge technology, real-time market information, and AI-powered pest detection to improve crop yields and profitability.

Our Vision

To become the leading agricultural technology platform in Malawi, connecting farmers with modern farming practices, market opportunities, and a supportive community.

Key Features

AI Pest Detection

Advanced machine learning for identifying crop diseases and pests

Market Prices

Real-time market data and price tracking for agricultural products

Weather Forecast

Accurate weather predictions to help with farming decisions
