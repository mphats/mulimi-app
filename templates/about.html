About Mulimi App

Empowering smallholder farmers with actionable insights, modern tools, and a supportive community.

Our Mission

Mulimi app helps farmers make better decisions by providing AI-powered pest diagnosis, real-time market prices, localized weather forecasts, and a vibrant community for knowledge sharing. Our goal is to increase productivity and improve livelihoods.

Our Vision

A resilient, data-driven agriculture ecosystem where every farmer—regardless of scale— can access modern tools and trusted information to grow healthier crops and earn more.

What You Get
AI Pest Diagnosis

Upload images and get instant diagnosis with treatment advice.

Market Prices

Track commodity prices to sell at the right time and place.

Weather Forecasts

Plan farming activities with localized and timely forecasts.

Community Forum

Ask questions, get advice, and share experiences.

How It Works
  1. Sign up or log in to your account.
  2. Use the Pest Diagnosis page to analyze crop images.
  3. Check Market Prices and Weather to plan your activities.
  4. Engage in the Community to learn and share knowledge.
